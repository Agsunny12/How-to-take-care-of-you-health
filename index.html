About Hygiene

Hygiene, the process that makes a person take care of their health, their appearance, their cleanliness to avoid contracting diseases or viruses, to clean the dirt, to conduct themselves in a healthy way in the society in which they live. Hygiene is a complex system of more or less simple actions that each individual must carry out on his own, ie hygiene is not a responsibility of the State or professionals (as can be maintained a controlled epidemic) if Not that it is pure and exclusive responsibility of the person.

Steps to maintain hygiene:



Wash your hands before and after eating food
Do not touch dirty things
Take Bath Daily
Brush your teeth every day and night
Always carry a sanitizer when you are outside
